Frequently Asked Question

1. What types of bikes do you offer?

We offer a wide range of bikes, including road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, electric bikes, and kids' bikes, catering to various cycling needs and preferences.

2. Do you offer test rides?

Yes, we encourage customers to take test rides to ensure they find a bike that is comfortable and suits their riding style. Please contact our showroom to schedule a test ride.

3. What brands do you carry?

We carry a variety of reputable brands known for their quality and performance, such as Trek, Giant, Specialized, Cannondale, and many more.

4. Do you provide bike fitting services?

Absolutely, our trained staff can provide professional bike fitting services to ensure your bike is adjusted to fit your body perfectly, enhancing comfort and performance.

5. What financing options are available?

We offer various financing options tailored to fit your budget, including installment plans, credit card payments, and financing through third-party providers.

6. Do you offer maintenance and repair services?

Yes, our experienced mechanics provide comprehensive maintenance and repair services to keep your bike in top condition. We also offer tune-ups, part replacements, and warranty repairs.

7. Can I trade in my old bike?

Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer competitive prices for pre-owned bikes. Please bring your bike to our showroom for a free evaluation and trade-in estimate.

8. Do you sell bike accessories and gear?

Yes, we carry a wide selection of bike accessories and gear, including helmets, locks, lights, apparel, and cycling shoes, to enhance your riding experience and safety.

9. What is your return and exchange policy?

We have a flexible return and exchange policy allowing customers to return or exchange their bike within a specified period if it is unused and in its original condition. Please refer to our policy for more details.

10. Do you offer bike assembly and delivery services?

Yes, we provide professional bike assembly and delivery services to ensure your new bike is set up correctly and ready to ride. Charges may apply depending on the location and complexity of assembly.
